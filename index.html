Hello, I’m Steph.

My home is Auckland, New Zealand.

Background

I’m a graphic/UI Designer, budding Front-End Web Developer and Apple fanatic. I have a Bachelor of Design (Hons) majoring in Visual Communications, and have worked as a Junior UI designer. Upon completion of the Treehouse Techdegree, I undertake my Master of Design.

My strengths lie in layout, typography, creative concept and my sharp attention to detail.

I am also a passionate educator and teach by day. Seven years teaching experience, in independent and collaborative/modern learning environments, has equipped me with strong skills in communication, collaboration, innovation, planning, project management and positive relationship management.

Goals

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Sass
  • JavaScript
  • Small Business
  • Digital Marketing

I aspire to create impactful, beautiful, intuitive and useful digital interfaces. I will do so by coupling my clean, simple and contemporary design principles with cutting-edge design concepts, trends and techniques.

My career goals are to work as a freelance designer and tertiary web design educator.
